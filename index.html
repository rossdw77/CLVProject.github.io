01 — About

The work is in the details.

I'm a data scientist with a background in customer analytics and predictive modeling. My work centers on building models that go beyond performance metrics to deliver something actually useful: interpretable, actionable outputs that connect directly to business decisions.

I care about the architecture decisions that happen before the first line of model code. Segmentation strategy, feature design, and validation frameworks are where most of the real thinking lives.

Currently building out a portfolio of end-to-end ML projects with an emphasis on model interpretability via SHAP and thoughtful experimental design.
